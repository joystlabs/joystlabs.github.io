## AI Processing

LoopKnit AI: Pattern Maker uses Google Gemini API services provided by Google LLC to generate AI-powered knitting and crochet pattern results.

When users use AI-powered features, the following data may be transmitted to Google Gemini services:

* User text prompts
* Uploaded images
* Image generation instructions

This data is transmitted solely for the purpose of generating AI-powered results requested by the user.

The app does not use uploaded content for advertising, user profiling, facial recognition, identity verification, or biometric analysis.

The app does not use user-provided content to train its own machine learning models.

Before any image or prompt is sent to Google Gemini services, the app presents a disclosure explaining what data will be shared and asks for the user’s explicit consent.

If the user declines, no image, prompt, or related data is transmitted to Google Gemini services.

Uploaded images and prompts are processed temporarily for AI generation purposes. The app does not permanently store user-uploaded images on its own servers unless explicitly stated within the app functionality.
